BMI

A simple way to measure obesity is the body mass index (BMI). It is calculated by dividing a person's weight in kilograms by the square of their height in meters. But don't worry, we do it for you. PROTEIN COUNTER

Proteins are found in every cell in the body. Our bodies need protein from the food we eat to build and maintain bones, muscles, and skin. It is important to get enough protein in the diet. You should eat protein every day, because the body doesn't store it the same way it stores fat or carbohydrates.
